About Ellis Patrick:

I am a Senior Lecturer in the School of Mathematics and Statistics and a faculty member at The Westmead Institute for Medical Research. My research interests center on the use and development of statistical methodology to analyse high-dimensional experiments and studies that measure the behaviour of thousands of molecules, cells, samples or subjects. Given this, I place a large value on the subtlety of translating between biological and statistical concepts to form suitable and targeted hypotheses.
